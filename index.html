English A1 Review

Simple Present - Verb "to be"

1. Laura (be) a good student.

2. They (be) from England.

3. She and I (be) members of the soccer team.

4. I (be) very good at math.

5. George (be) an excellent teacher.

6. The mouse (not be) very big.

7. My parents (not be) young anymore.

8. My girlfriend and I (not be) fans of Coldplay.

9. I (not be) a patient person.

10. Lisa (not be) from Belgium.

9. There (not be) only one doctor to take care of us.

10. There (not be) hotels in Marseille to choose from.
